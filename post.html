SEO

SEO means ‘Search Engine Optimization.’ In ‘simple English’ it means to code or edit a website that Google can ‘read’ easily, and your goal is to rank higher for your search term (this is a very simplified answer).

Off-page happens outside of your website. This is all the activities that you and others do for the site to rank higher in the search engine results.

In this article we are going to focus on On-page SEO we will get into off-page SEO later.
