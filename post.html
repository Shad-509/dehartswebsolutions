SEO

SEO can be broken down into 2 sections: On-page SEO and off-page SEO. On page happens within the website. This is all the optimizing you do on your website to try and rank in the #1 spot on the search engine results. Off-page happens outside of your website. This is all the activities that you and others do for the site to rank higher in the search engine results. In this article we are going to focus on On-page SEO we will get into off-page SEO later.

SEO means ‘Search Engine Optimization.’ In ‘simple English’ it means to code or edit a website that Google can ‘read’ easily, and your goal is to rank higher for your search term (this is a very simplified answer).
